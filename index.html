Genghan Zhang (张庚瀚)

zgh23[at]stanford[dot]edu

I’m a second-year Ph.D. student in Stanford University Department of Computer Science advised by Professor Kunle Olukotun. I was also privileged to work with Professor Fredrik Kjolstad and Professor Azalia Mirhoseini.

I graduated from Tsinghua University in 2023 with a degree in Eletronic Engineering. At Tsinghua, I was fortunate enough to do research at NICS-EFC Lab and IDEAL Lab.

Research: My research interests lie in better programming models and systems for domain-specific architectures. I am also interested in optimizing GPU kernels for emerging applications, including sparse and recurrent neural networks.
