### Gaussian Process Regression

This is the project of Probability and Stochastic Processes(2) Autumn 2021 by [Hao Zhang](http://web.ee.tsinghua.edu.cn/zhanghao/zh_EN/index.htm). I used Gaussian Process Regression(GPR) to predict the stock price of AAPL, HPE, and INTC. The code and the report can be found [here](https://github.com/zhang677/A-case-study-of-GPR).

### 5-stage Pipeline 

This is the project of Fundamental Experiment of Digital Logic and Processor Summer 2021, which is the experiment course of Fundamental of digital logic and processor Spring 2021 by [Prof. Xueqing Li](http://nics.ee.tsinghua.edu.cn/people/Xueqing/). The code can be found at [this repo](https://github.com/zhang677/MIPS-Processor-Project). It took me about two weeks to finish this project. Debugging verilog code was challenging but interesting. Implementing pipeline has some subtle details different from the [book](https://ict.iitk.ac.in/wp-content/uploads/CS422-Computer-Architecture-ComputerOrganizationAndDesign5thEdition2014.pdf). The video is [here](https://youtu.be/Yugr0-O5LlE).

### Mini electromotor

This is a mid-term project of Electromagnetic Field and Wave Spring 2021. [Prof. Cheng Ma](https://rachmaninov-ma.wixsite.com/mysite) advised that we could use this project to illustrate Lorentz Force. I put three pieces of magnet under a battery. And I bent the copper wire to a gate-like shape. By the way, it's pretty tough to bend the copper wire. The video is [here](https://youtu.be/cI_hnI9n9gQ).
